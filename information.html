Proportion and Scale

Proportion is the ratio between the size of one part to another,and scale is how the size of one object relates to another or to the space in which it is placed.For instances,a large over stuffed sectional in a small room will be out of scale.Some propotional relationships are pleasing than others.The acient Greeks came up with Golden Section,which sought to reduce all propotion to a simple formulae:The ratio of smaller section to larger section should be same as that of larger section to whole.This propotion is present in nature,and artist and architects have used it well.



TRICKS


1.Paint:Though painting is the cheapest investment you can make,it's also the one with the most dramatic result.Go ahead and put some color on those walls for walls for fresh-and inexpensive-new look.


2.Do It Yourself:The biggest budget buster is hiring others to do things you could do.Consider what you'll save by taking on some relatively simple projects:painting,tiling,installing a new floor,sewing curtain panels or throw pillows-whatever you feel up to trying.

3.Call in Family and Friends:You may not have a wad of cash or a lot of home-improvement know-how,but you have other resources that can help get your decorating project off the ground:The people you know.Call on a group of relatives,friends and neighbours who can supply the muscle to rip down the wallpaneling,roll on paint or assemble a room of flat-packed furniture.just don't forget to feed them lunch.


4.Shop Secondhand stores:Thrift stores,consignment shops,church rummage sales,onlion auction sites,estate sales and even salvage yards offers a bounty of discount discorting booty.Look for furniture with solid construction and classic lines that new uphoistery or pint will bring back to life.
